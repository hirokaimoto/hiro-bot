The goal of the study was to compare different statistical models and machine learning techniques to create buy and sell systems for the Bitcoin price quotation. The models and systems compared were 4 models of the first system ARIMA, MA, PROPHET and AR, and 1 case of the Voting System, both with daily periodicity. The methodology proposed used the closing price of Bitcoin for both periodicities, and statistical and machine learning techniques as predictors. Different ways were presented to evaluate the results in an intrinsic and extrinsic way.
