The goal of the study was to use data containing daily and hourly Bitcoin quotations to train models according to their own parameterization and then use them to make predictions and analyze them intrinsically and extrinsically. The results were then evaluated and compared. Specifically, the data was collected from Kaggle (2020) for the period of 08/10/2015 to 09/04/2020 for both daily (1D) and hourly (1H) periods. The variables found were opening, maximum, minimum, and closing prices for the given period. Data exploration consisted of decomposition and autocorrelation of the time series which helped extract useful information for model construction. The data was then prepared by separating 80% for training (08/10/2015 to 15/05/2019) and 20% for testing (16/05/2019 to 09/04/2020) using only the closing price as input for the models since this is most relevant to investors and traders.
